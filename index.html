Supported sites include YouTube, Vimeo, and thousands of others.

With YouMacro you can:

(1) Download and watch 4k videos on your device including mobile. Many sites, including YouTube, deliberately limit the content resolution on mobile.

(2) Reduce your data plan usage when playing videos repeatedly. Repeated plays will read from your local storage.

(3) Avoid watching any ads. YouMacro will not download any ads.

(4) Watch videos without wifi or a data plan. Handy, when you find yourself in a location without wifi or cell service.
